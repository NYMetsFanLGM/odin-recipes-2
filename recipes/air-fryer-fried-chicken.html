Air Fryer Fried Chicken recipe

Recipe Description

This recipe involves first preparing the marinade. Once prepared, dump the chicken in it and then place meat in fridge overnight.

The next day, you then cover the chicken in the seasonings and then cook in the air fryer.

marinade ingredients

  • 1/2 cup hot sauce
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk

seasoning ingredients

  • 3/4 cup of all purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons of garlic salt
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon goya adobo seasoning with pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • Non-stick spray for air fryer

Instructions

  1. mix the marinade ingredients in a bowl
  2. place chicken in marinade, place in ziplock bag, and then place in fridge overnight
  3. set air fryer to 390 degrees and leave running for a few minutes to heat up
  4. cover chicken in seasoning ingredients
  5. place chicken in air fryer skin side down for 13 minutes
  6. open air fryer and spray any flour spots on top of chicken
  7. flip chicken over, spray again over any flour spots, and then place back in air fryer for 12 more minutes
