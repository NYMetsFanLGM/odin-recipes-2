Thanksgiving Ham

Recipe Description

This recipe involves preparing a glaze to brush over the ham when finished. The ham should be cooked for 15 or 20 minutes per pound depending on instructions for the cook ham bought.

For this recipe, I used orange juice instead of pineapple juice and ground cinnamon instead of ground ginger. I also used honey mustard instead of Dijon mustard.

Ingredients

ingredients for pre-cooked ham glaze

  • 1 large ham
  • 2 tablespoons honey mustard
  • 1/4 cup orange juice

ingredients for post-cooked ham glaze

  • 1/2 cup orange juice
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons honey mustard
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 2 teaspoons cornstarch

instructions

  1. preheat oven to 325o degrees
  2. brush ham with pre-cooked ham glaze
  3. place ham flat side down and cover tightly with foil then place in oven.
  4. while ham is cooking, prepare post-cooked ham glaze in sauce pan and bring to a boil
  5. once boiled, turn heat down and remove for glaze to cool
  6. 15 minutes before ham is done, remove from oven and turn oven up to 425 degrees
  7. while oven is heating up, brush ham with8 post-cooked glaze
  8. put back ham in oven for another 15 minutes
