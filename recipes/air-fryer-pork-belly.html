Air Fryer Pork Belly

Recipe Description

For this recipe, first cut up the pork belly into pieces in order to fit into the air fryer. Once cut up, season up the pork and then place in fridge overnight before air frying.

Ingredients

  • onion powder
  • garlic powder
  • garlic salt
  • goya adobo seasoning with pepper
  • 1 packet of Goya Sason seasoning

instructions

  1. set air fryer to 400 degrees and leave it on for a few minutes to wrm up
  2. spray inside air fryer with non-stick spray
  3. place pieces of the pork belly with skin on its side
  4. cook for 20 minutes and flip the pork halfway
